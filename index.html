MY STORY

My journey into programming began with a passion for typesetting fansub videos in my free time. As a detail-oriented typesetter, I always aimed to deliver the best quality products to enhance the viewer experience, requiring a significant amount of time and effort. The more I engaged in typesetting, the more challenges I encountered, fueling my desire to create tools that could address these issues and simplify the typesetting process.

After sharing my first automation scripts with the public and receiving much thanks and support, I gained a tremendous amount of motivation and passion for programming. Since then, I've honed my skills by crafting several automation scripts that address typesetters' pain points, enabling them to produce higher quality videos in much shorter time.

In addition to sharing automation scripts, I've also created tutorials on subtitle typesetting, including writing code to generate effects. It brings me immense happiness to know that my programs and tutorials make many people's lives easier.
